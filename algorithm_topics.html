I spent a little time on the Springer Publishing site searching for some good textbooks on Algorithms.So I searched for Algorithms in Textbooks only.

  • Algorithms for Data Science by Steele et al (2016)
  • Algorithms and Data Structures:Foundations and Probabilistic Methods for Design and Analysis by Helmut Knebl (2020)
  • Big Data Analytics: Systems, Algorithms, Applications by Prabhu et al (2019)
  • Bioinformatics: An Introduction by Jeremy Ramsden (2021)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms with Python by Lee and Hubbard (2015)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms with Scala:A Practitioner's Approach with Emphasis on Functional Programming by Bhim Upadhyaya (2019)
  • Distributed and Sequential Algorithms for Bioinformatics by K Erciyes(2015)
  • Elementary Functions:Algorithms and Implementation by Jean-Michel Muller (2016)
  • Guide to Programming and Algorithms Using R by Özgür Ergül (2013)
  • The Power of Algorithms:Inspiration and Examples in Everyday Life edited by Ausiello and Petreschi (2013)
  • Simulation Algorithms for Computational Systems Biology by Marchetti et al (2017)
